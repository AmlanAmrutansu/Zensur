## 📌 Overview  

Zensure is an AI-powered **parametric insurance platform** designed to protect the income of **quick-commerce delivery partners (Zepto, Blinkit, Instamart)** from external disruptions such as weather, pollution, and infrastructure failures.

> ⚡ **No Claims. No Delays. Fully Automated Payouts.**

---

## 🎯 Problem  

Quick-commerce riders face **high income volatility**:

- 🌧 Rain → fewer deliveries  
- 🌡 Heat → reduced working hours  
- 🌫 Pollution → unsafe conditions  
- 🚫 Curfew → no work  

### 🔴 Impact  

- 30%–70% income loss  
- No financial safety net  
- Immediate instability  

---

## 💡 Solution  

Zensure introduces a **parametric insurance system**:  
➡️ **Event → Detection → Validation → Instant Payout**  

✅ No manual claims  
✅ No waiting

## ⚙️ Parametric Triggers  

| Trigger | Condition | Outcome |
|--------|----------|--------|
| 🌧 Rainfall | > 70mm + delivery drop | Payout |
| 🌡 Heatwave | > 42°C | Payout |
| 🌫 AQI | > 400 | Payout |
| 🚫 Curfew | Movement restricted | Payout |
| ⚡ Platform Outage | Zero orders | Payout |

---

## 🤖 AI System  

### 🔹 Risk Prediction  
- Model: Random Forest  
- Output: Risk Score (0.5 – 2.0)  

### 🔹 Premium Engine  
`Premium = Base Rate × Risk Score`  

| Risk Level | Premium |
|-----------|--------|
| Low | ₹10 |
| Medium | ₹15 |
| High | ₹20 |

### 🔹 Loss Estimation  
`Expected Income - Actual Income = Loss`  

### 🔹 Fraud Detection  
- Model: Isolation Forest  
- Detects:
  - GPS spoofing  
  - Duplicate claims  
  - Abnormal patterns
